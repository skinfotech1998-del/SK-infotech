Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access my CCTV remotely?

You can access your CCTV cameras remotely through our mobile app or web portal. Contact our support team for setup assistance.

What if my camera stops working?

First, check the power connection and cables. If the issue persists, contact our support team immediately for quick resolution.

Do you provide AMC services?

Yes, we offer comprehensive Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) to ensure your security systems are always operational.

How long does repair take?

Most repairs are completed within 24-48 hours. Emergency repairs are prioritized and handled immediately.

Is support available on weekends?

Yes, our support team is available 24/7, including weekends and holidays for emergency assistance.

How do I upgrade my system?

Contact our sales team to discuss upgrade options. We'll assess your current system and recommend the best solutions.
